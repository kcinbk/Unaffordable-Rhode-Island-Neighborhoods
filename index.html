Rhode Island's Unaffordable Neigborhoods

Between 2021 and 2023, Rhode Island recorded over 23,000 eviction filings, according to data tracked by The Eviction Lab at Princeton University. And like much of the US, a large share of renters say they are experience rent burdened, paying at least 30 percent of their household income for rent. Some even pay rent takes up 50 percent or more of their paychecks each month.
